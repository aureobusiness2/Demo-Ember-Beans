Our Story: The Ember & Bean Journey

Ember & Bean began with a simple dream: to create a space where the rich aroma of craft coffee meets the tranquil pace of slow mornings. Born from a passion for exceptional beans and a desire to foster genuine connection, our journey started in the heart of Naga City.

We believe that a cup of coffee is more than just a beverage; it's an experience. It's the quiet moment before the day begins, the shared laughter with friends, and the fuel for creative minds. Every bean is carefully selected, every roast meticulously perfected, and every pour crafted with intention. We source ethically, support local, and strive to bring you not just coffee, but a ritual.

From our cozy corners to our vibrant community events, Ember & Bean is a haven for dreamers, doers, and anyone seeking a moment of warmth and inspiration. Join us, and become a part of our story.
